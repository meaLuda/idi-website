Decision Intelligence Design Academy

The Institute of Design and Innovation (IDI) recognizes the need for a new generation of decision-makers who are ready to lead with intelligence, innovation, and impact.

To meet this need, IDI proudly presents transformative design innovation academy with programs designed to build decision-making capacities across sectors.

Our Key Objectives

  • •

    Empower Decsion-Making: Equip participants with cutting-edge decision intelligence skills to navigate complex challenges and drive meaningful change in their respective fields.

  • •

    Strengthen Cross-Sector Collaboration: Facilitate strategic partnerships between public institutions, private enterprises, and civic organizations to co-create impactful solutions.

  • •

    Drive Innovation for Impact: Enable participants to apply decision intelligence methodologies to real-world challenges, ensuring solutions are data-driven, ethical, and sustainable.

  • •

    Scale Transformative Solutions: Develop replicable and adaptable decision-making frameworks that can be implemented across industries and geographies for long-term impact.

DID Programs

Our Decision Intelligence programs are designed to transform complexity into clarity by blending data, design, and ethics to empower better decision-making.

Through a mix of curated learning, real-world case studies, and hands-on design sprints, participants explore life-centered design, behavioral science, AI, and data analytics to tackle complex challenges. Whether you're building foundational knowledge or applying advanced strategies at the executive level, our programs offer interactive learning, peer exchange, and mentorship—equipping professionals with the tools to drive meaningful impact across sectors.

A structured approach to transform how organizations make decisions through data, design, and deliberate action.

Sense → Frame → Model → Decide → Act → Learn

This continuous cycle guides practitioners from spotting signals to embedding insight-driven actions and improvements in their decision-making processes.

Each stage builds on the last:

  • • sensing real-world data,
  • • framing clear questions,
  • • modeling evidence-based options,
  • • deciding with confidence,
  • • acting through purposeful implementation, and
  • • learning from outcomes to restart the cycle stronger.

Mastering this rhythm equips teams to make smarter, faster, and more responsible decisions in any context.
